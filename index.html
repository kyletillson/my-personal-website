Why I chose this field

I originally went into trade school to become an automotive technician at Wyotech, in Laramie, Wyoming. Unfortunately, during my time there I had a cavernous malformation. It put me out for a long time and I had to get brain surgery to remove it. After my recovery, I still tried to pursue a career in the automotive field. I ended up getting a job at Autonation and worked there for about half a year before my body and brain couldn’t take it anymore. I then decided it would be best to choose a field that didn’t require me to put my body through constant strenuous work all the time. I started to get certifications through Comptia, before I decided I wanted to become a software engineer.
